Who Are We?

Welcome to Academic Society. Our server is a respectful and calm space that combines focused studying with entertainment. We aim to provide an environment that helps you accomplish your tasks and organize your day, while also offering a place to relax and play with respectful members.

The server offers quiet study rooms for deep focus, gaming rooms for your break time, and dedicated bots to help you manage your time (like Pomodoro) and listen to the Holy Quran.

Whether you are looking for a highly focused study environment or want to unwind with games in a respectful community, you have found your place with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the voice channels?

You just need to join our Discord server, read the rules, and you will automatically get the member role to access all voice channels.

Is the server only for studying?

Not at all! While we have dedicated study zones, we also have gaming channels, respectful general chat, and regular community events.

How do I become a moderator?

We occasionally open applications for staff. Be active, help others, and keep an eye on the announcements channel!
